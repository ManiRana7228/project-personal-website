HUNZA

Hunza is a mountainous region located in the northernmost part of Pakistan. It is situated in the Gilgit-Baltistan territory, bordering Afghanistan and China. The region is known for its natural beauty, including snow-capped peaks, glaciers, and crystal-clear rivers. The people of Hunza are predominantly of Burusho ethnicity and speak the Burushaski language. They are known for their longevity, with some members of the community living well into their 90s and even reaching over 100 years of age. This has led to interest in their diet and lifestyle, with some attributing their longevity to a diet rich in whole foods, including fruits, vegetables, nuts, and grains.

KASHMIR

Kashmir is a region located in the northern part of the Indian subcontinent, bordering India, Pakistan, and China. The region is known for its natural beauty, including snow-capped mountains, scenic valleys, and serene lakes. Kashmir has a complex political history and has been a disputed territory between India and Pakistan since the partition of India in 1947. The region has been the site of conflict and violence, with both India and Pakistan claiming ownership of the region. The people of Kashmir are ethnically diverse, with a majority of the population being Muslim.

KHANPUR DAM

Khanpur Dam is a large earth-filled dam located in the Haripur District of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province in Pakistan. It was completed in 1983 and was built on the Haro River, which is a tributary of the Indus River. The dam has a storage capacity of 110,000 acre-feet and is used for irrigation, drinking water supply, and hydroelectric power generation. It provides water to the Rawalpindi and Islamabad areas and supports agriculture in the surrounding region. In addition to its practical uses, Khanpur Dam is a popular tourist destination and is known for its scenic beauty and recreational opportunities.

SKARDU

Skardu is a town located in the Gilgit-Baltistan region of Pakistan, nestled in the Karakoram Range at an altitude of 2,438 meters (7,999 ft) above sea level. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, including snow-capped peaks, glaciers, and the Indus River flowing through it. Skardu is a popular tourist destination, particularly for adventure enthusiasts. The region offers opportunities for trekking, mountaineering, and rock climbing. The nearby mountains, including K2, the world's second-highest peak, make Skardu a hub for mountaineering expeditions.

DUBAI

Dubai is a city located in the United Arab Emirates (UAE), on the southeast coast of the Persian Gulf. It is the largest and most populous city in the UAE and is known for its modern architecture, luxurious shopping, and vibrant nightlife. Dubai is a hub for international trade, with a strong economy driven by industries such as tourism, finance, and real estate. The city has developed rapidly over the past few decades, with towering skyscrapers and innovative infrastructure projects, such as the world's largest shopping mall and the Burj Khalifa, the world's tallest building.

Gabbin Jabba

Gabin Jabba is a scenic hill station located in the Swat district of the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province in Pakistan. It is known for its picturesque beauty, with rolling hills, lush green forests, and breathtaking views of the surrounding mountain ranges. Gabin Jabba is a popular destination for adventure enthusiasts, with opportunities for trekking, camping, and hiking. The area is home to several trekking trails that offer stunning views of the surrounding landscape. The nearby river Swat also provides opportunities for fishing and boating.
