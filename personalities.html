In addition to the natural beauty, Islamabad is home to many modern and innovative buildings, including the Faisal Mosque, which is one of the largest mosques in the world and an impressive feat of architecture. Overall, Islamabad's blend of natural beauty and modern architecture make it a unique and stunning city to visit or call home..

JAUN ELIA

Jaun Elia (1931-2002) was a Pakistani poet, philosopher, and scholar known for his unconventional and revolutionary poetry that challenged traditional conventions and norms. Elia was born in Uttar Pradesh, India, and moved to Pakistan after the partition in 1947. He wrote in both Urdu and English and was known for his profound thoughts and ideas on various topics such as love, politics, and social issues. Elia's poetry was characterized by its complexity and depth, and he was known for his use of metaphors and allegories to convey his ideas. His work often reflected the struggles of the common people and the societal injustices they faced. In addition to his poetry, Elia was also a respected scholar and translator, and he wrote several books on various topics such as philosophy, literature, and linguistics.
